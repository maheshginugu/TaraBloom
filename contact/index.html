Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I place an order?

Simply click “Add to Cart” on any product and you’ll be redirected to WhatsApp to complete your order.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept UPI, bank transfer, Paytm, PhonePe, and Cash on Delivery (COD) for all orders.

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery takes 5-7 business days. Express delivery (2-3 days) is available at extra charge.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns only for damaged products. To request a return, you must provide a clear unboxing video showing the damage. Returns without an unboxing video cannot be processed.
